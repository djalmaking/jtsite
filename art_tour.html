Art tour on the streets

Johannesburg is a canvas for artists from all over the world. We take you through many artists who are situated in the different districts of Joburg. They will showcase their craft and share their personal life stories behind the art.

On this art tour, you are given an amount of time to spend and paint something with a South African artist, you capture your own experience of the city and your journey. Paint a masterpiece that will mark your journey as one of the most memorable, during your visit in Johannesburg.

The artists are from diverse cultural backgrounds, which enables them to paint a story that is understood differently. Perspective is the name of the game, each artists perspective is usually a personal dialogue between themselves, the way they were brought up and the changes they go through in the city.

Join us and be a part of the painters narrative in Joburg. We take you wherever there's a seemingly mundane culture and uncover rich stories and history that no one pays attention to.
